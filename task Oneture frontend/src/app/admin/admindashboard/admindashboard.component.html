The Oneture

Oneture is an incubator with a specific focus on sports and technology. Our mission is to select talented and unique teams to invest on, and help them to grow their businesses. We believe this choice to be strategic because of a few reasons:

Sports industry is growing at very fast pace in terms of innovation.

Innovation (we are the first Sports Tech Incubator in Italy and there are only a few similar entities in Europe).

We believe that the sports tech industry will be able to attract young and creative talents more than other business industries.
